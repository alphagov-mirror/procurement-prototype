Audit consultancy Find a supplier through the Management Consultancy framework (RM3745)

When you buy through this framework under the standard terms and conditions:

  • you can agree a fixed price with the supplier, or one based on risk and reward or time and materials
  • you’ll usually own any intellectual property that’s created

View all suppliers on this framework

Next steps

  • (Optional) Run an expression of interest
  • Write your requirements
  • Put out a tender
  • Evaluate suppliers responses
  • Notify suppliers of the outcome
  • Agree contract using call off order form

Direct award

In some cases you may be able to award your contract directly to a supplier (step 6). This might mean you don’t get the best value. Check with your head of procurement or legal adviser first.
